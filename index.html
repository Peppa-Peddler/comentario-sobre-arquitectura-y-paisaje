Comments on architecture and landscape

This ongoing project is based on a series of compositions inspired by maps, blueprints and landscapes of the city of Lima, especially of the southern coastline.

Each drawing is presented as a comment/note/thought through which I interpret and/or reimagine the relationship between coastal landscapes and its architecture.
